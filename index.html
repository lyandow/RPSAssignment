Rock Paper Scissors

The Rules

In Rock Paper Scissors, you enter either rock, paper, or scissors. The computer will do the same, and your choice will be compared to the computer's. In order to win, your choice must 'beat' the computer's. Rock beats scissors but loses to paper, paper beats rock but loses to scissors, and scissors beats paper but loses to rock.
